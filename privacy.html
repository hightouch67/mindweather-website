Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 1, 2026

MindWeather ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Do Not Collect

MindWeather is designed with privacy as its foundation. We do not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal information or data from our users. This includes:

  • Names, email addresses, or contact information
  • Location data
  • Device information or identifiers
  • Usage analytics or tracking data
  • Journal entries or mental health data

Local Storage Only

All your journal entries, mood data, and personal information remain stored locally on your device. MindWeather does not require an internet connection to function, and we have no servers or cloud storage where your data could be sent.

No Third-Party Services

MindWeather does not integrate with any third-party services, analytics platforms, or advertising networks. Your data stays completely under your control.

Data Security

Since all data is stored locally on your device, security depends on your device's security measures. We recommend using device-level security features like passcodes, biometric authentication, and regular backups.

Children's Privacy

MindWeather is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect any information from children under 13.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the app and on this website.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us through our GitHub repository: https://github.com/hightouch67/mindweather-website/issues
